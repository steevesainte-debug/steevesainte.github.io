Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need gardening experience?

Not at all! We welcome beginners. We'll provide resources to help you get started, and you'll be part of a community of gardeners who can help.

What if I don't have a backyard?

You can still participate! Find a homeowner who has unused yard space, garden there, and split the rewards 80/20 (you get 80%, they get 20%).

How big does my garden need to be?

Minimum 10 sq ft (about 3ft x 3ft - roughly the size of a raised bed). Maximum 200 sq ft for pilot rewards.

What can I grow?

Any edible plants! Vegetables (tomatoes, peppers, lettuce), herbs (basil, cilantro), or fruit. Must be food-producing plants.

How do I get my YARD tokens?

You'll need a Polygon wallet (we'll help you set this up). After your weekly photos are approved, tokens are sent automatically to your wallet.

Is this a cryptocurrency investment?

No. YardCoin is a utility token earned through gardening work. It's not sold, marketed as an investment, or traded. This is about rewarding community food production.

How long is the pilot?

12 weeks, starting in [MONTH]. We're looking for 10-15 founding participants to test the concept.

What if I miss a week?

That's okay! You only get rewards for weeks where you submit proof. If you miss a week, just resume the following week.
